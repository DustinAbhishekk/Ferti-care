Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What's your return policy?

Unopened products in original packaging can be returned within 30 days for a full refund. Special order items may have different return policies. Please contact our support team for specific return instructions.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also track your order by logging into your account on our website.

Do you offer bulk discounts?

Yes, we offer quantity discounts for most products. Discounts start at orders over $500. Contact our sales team for custom quotes on large orders.

How do I contact an agronomist?

Our agricultural experts are available by phone at (800) 123-4567 ext. 2, or you can select "Agricultural Advice" as your subject when submitting the contact form.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and agricultural credit accounts (subject to approval). We also accept checks for established customers.

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we only ship within the United States and Canada. For international inquiries, please contact our sales team for possible arrangements.
