Our Story

How Ferti-Care became a leader in agricultural solutions

2008

Humble Beginnings

Founded by agricultural engineer Mark Johnson, Ferti-Care started as a small fertilizer blending facility serving local farms in Iowa.

2012

First Research Partnership

Collaborated with Iowa State University to develop our signature slow-release fertilizer formula, still used in our premium products today.

2015

National Expansion

Opened distribution centers in 5 states and launched our e-commerce platform, making our products available nationwide.

2018

Sustainable Farming Initiative

Introduced our organic product line and launched educational programs to promote sustainable farming practices.

2023

Global Reach

Now serving over 50,000 farmers across North America with plans for international expansion.
